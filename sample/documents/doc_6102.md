TECHNICAL MEMORANDUM

TO: Meteorological Modeling Departments
FROM: Meteorological Standards Committee
SUBJECT: Standardization of Radiometry Variables for Meteorological Modeling

Effective immediately, the Meteorological Standards Committee has established a standardized scientific designation for the integrated value of downward solar flux as utilized in meteorological modeling applications. To ensure consistency across all environmental simulations, the technical name for the calculated sum of solar energy is hereby defined as Global Horizontal Irradiance.

The adoption of this nomenclature for the standardized downward flux completes the formal definition of this environmental variable. This standard shall be applied to all flux measurements, such as those recorded at the Arid Heights Research Annex in The High Plains Meridian, Federative Republic of Althea, to maintain rigorous modeling integrity and uniform data terminology.
