INTERNAL MEMO

TO: Technical Operations Distribution List
FROM: Revenue Assurance Department
DATE: December 14, 2024
SUBJECT: Internal Q4 Data Integrity Bulletin: Nuance Cosmetic Lab Sales Anomaly

This bulletin serves to formally document a software bug identified within the Nuance Cosmetic Lab reporting system. Recent diagnostic reviews indicate that the timing of this technical event has shifted to late 2024.

Investigations confirm that a specific technical glitch resulted in a duplicated sales volume effect across the regional dashboard. Consequently, the recorded data for this period is officially designated as an anomaly. Stakeholders are advised that these figures provide no predictive insight for forward-looking projections.

Per the reporting scope of this department, this notice is strictly limited to the technical reporting error and its immediate data impact. No further information regarding financial implications or code remediation is included herein.

Ref: RAD-2024-092-A
