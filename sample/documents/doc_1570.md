INTERNAL MEMORANDUM

TO: All Staff, Cosmetic Research Division, Operations, and Brand Management
FROM: Operations & Brand Management Department
DATE: December 14, 2024
SUBJECT: 2025-2026 Operational Strategy and Brand Integration

This memorandum outlines the logistical adjustments, training schedules, and visual identity updates for the upcoming 2025-2026 fiscal cycle. This document serves as a guide for internal operational logistics and brand alignment.

I. Office Supply Procurement Cycles (2025-2026)
To ensure continuity in daily operations, the procurement of office supplies will move to a standardized quarterly cycle. The 2025-2026 schedule is as follows:
- Q1 2025: January 15 submission deadline
- Q2 2025: April 10 submission deadline
- Q3 2025: July 15 submission deadline
- Q4 2025: October 10 submission deadline
- 2026 Cycle: Deadlines remain consistent with the 15th of the starting month of each quarter (or the nearest business day). Inventory audits will be conducted two weeks prior to each cycle start.

II. Internal Training Seminars: Cosmetic Research Staff
A specialized curriculum for the research wing has been developed to enhance internal technical capabilities. All research personnel are required to attend the following seminars:
- Seminar A: Advanced Formulation Standards (March 2025) – Focused on internal safety protocols.
- Seminar B: Raw Material Integrity (August 2025) – Reviewing internal sourcing standards.
- Seminar C: Data Integrity in Lab Testing (February 2026) – Focused on reporting and documentation.
- Seminar D: Collaborative R&D Pipelines (September 2026) – Integration of research workflows with operational management.
Registration for these sessions will be handled through the internal HR portal.

III. Visual Identity and Corporate Brand Updates
In alignment with the 2025-2026 strategy, the corporate branding has undergone specific color updates to modernize our visual identity. These updates must be applied to all internal documents, laboratory labels, and presentation templates effective immediately. 

The updated brand palette includes:
- Primary Corporate Blue: Updated to a deeper navy hue (Ref: Internal Palette #002D5B).
- Secondary Silver Accent: Updated to a matte metallic finish (Ref: Internal Palette #A2AAAD).
- Research Wing Highlight: A new designated teal for laboratory-specific signage (Ref: Internal Palette #008291).

Existing stationery and digital assets must be transitioned to these updated specifications by the end of Q1 2025. This memo is intended for internal departmental use and focuses strictly on operational logistics, research staff development, and visual identity updates. It contains no financial performance data, market forecasts, or external competitive analysis.
