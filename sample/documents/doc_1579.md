MEMORANDUM

TO: All Operations Staff
FROM: Operations Manager, Velvet Aura Apothecary
DATE: April 15, 2024
RE: Internal Q1 Performance Summary and Operations Update

As we transition into the second quarter of the year, this memorandum serves to reconcile our performance data from the initial months of 2024 and address the recent shifts in our operational volume.

Performance Review: Q1 2024
Based on our internal monitoring, Velvet Aura Apothecary observed a decrease in sales during the initial period of 2024-01-01 to 2024-03-31. This period was characterized by lower-than-anticipated activity across our primary channels. Our internal tracking systems, which record sales data at a daily frequency, consistently reflected this downward trend throughout the first three months of the year.

Operational Shift and Membership Launch
Following the conclusion of the first quarter, we have observed a significant change in our performance metrics. A permanent baseline shift in sales volume occurred starting 2024-04-01. This shift represents a fundamental change in our daily output levels compared to the previous quarter.

Management has identified the primary driver for this transition. The sustained increase in the sales baseline is attributed to the launch of our new membership program. Since the program’s introduction on April 1st, daily transaction volume has stabilized at a higher level, effectively offsetting the decline experienced during the Q1 period.

Conclusion
While the first quarter presented challenges regarding sales momentum, the current data suggests that the membership program has successfully established a new, higher threshold for our daily operations. We will continue to monitor daily sales data closely to ensure our operational capacity remains aligned with this permanent baseline shift. Please note that this summary is restricted to the reported sales decline in the first quarter and the subsequent impact of the membership program; further financial projections or external market analyses are not included in this update.
