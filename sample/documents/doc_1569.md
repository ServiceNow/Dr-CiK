Nuance Cosmetic Lab
Internal Memorandum

TO: All Staff, Nuance Cosmetic Lab
FROM: Facility Operations and IT Administration Department
DATE: October 15, 2025
SUBJECT: Winter 2025-2026 Operational & Facility Management Update

As we approach the Winter 2025-2026 season, the Facility Operations and IT Administration Department has finalized the upcoming schedule for maintenance, sanitation, and administrative adjustments. This memorandum outlines the specific logistical requirements and operational changes necessary to maintain facility standards through early 2026.

I. HARDWARE MAINTENANCE SCHEDULES (IT INFRASTRUCTURE)
To ensure data integrity and system reliability, the following hardware maintenance window for Nuance Cosmetic Lab servers has been established:

• Primary Data Server (NCL-Srv01): December 12–14, 2025. Comprehensive diagnostic and cooling system cleanup.
• Laboratory Management Systems (LMS-01/02): January 15, 2026. Hardware optimization and hardware component integrity checks.
• Backup Arrays: February 5, 2026. Routine hardware redundancy testing.

All server maintenance will occur during off-peak hours (22:00 – 04:00) to minimize disruption to active lab operations.

II. ROUTINE LABORATORY CLEANING PROTOCOLS
Environmental sterility remains a priority. The following routine cleaning protocols are scheduled to maintain compliance during the winter period:

• Deep Sterilization (Zones A & B): November 30, 2025. Full sanitization of all surfaces and ventilation grilles.
• Monthly Maintenance Wash: Scheduled for the third Sunday of December (2025), January (2026), and February (2026).
• Floor Resealing: February 18–19, 2026. High-traffic laboratory corridors will undergo specialized chemical cleaning and resealing.

Staff are requested to clear all portable equipment from benches prior to the 18:00 start time on these dates.

III. ADMINISTRATIVE CHANGES TO SHIFT ROTATION
To accommodate the winter period and ensure consistent facility coverage, the following administrative changes to the employee shift rotation system are effective from December 1, 2025, through March 1, 2026:

• Shift A (Morning): 06:00 – 14:30
• Shift B (Afternoon): 14:00 – 22:30
• Shift C (Overnight): 22:00 – 06:30

The overlapping 30-minute window between shifts has been implemented to facilitate comprehensive handovers between teams. Rotation assignments for the winter window will be published via the internal portal by the end of the current month.

NOTE: This update pertains strictly to internal operational logistics and maintenance planning for the Winter 2025-2026 window. This document does not contain financial reports, product research data, or long-term strategic forecasts.
