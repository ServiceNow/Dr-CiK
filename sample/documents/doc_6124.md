METEOROLOGICAL OPERATIONAL REPORT
Site: Arid Heights Research Annex
Subject: Daily Solar Irradiance Forecast Summary

OBSERVATIONS
Current monitoring data indicates that the Actual Global Horizontal Irradiance (GHI) at the Arid Heights Research Annex consistently trails just below the theoretical maximum. Local atmospheric conditions maintain a narrow margin of separation between the attenuated irradiance curve and the cloudless potential during all daylight hours.

24-HOUR FORECAST
The next daily cycle is expected to remain suppressed due to the established trend of trailing below theoretical potential values. Consequently, the midday peak for the upcoming cycle is forecast to remain below the 900 Watts per squared meter threshold.

Note: This report is restricted to site-specific observations and immediate forecasts for the Arid Heights Research Annex.
