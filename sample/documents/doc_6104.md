Solar Irradiance Modeling Specification Sheet: Case Study R1-R2

Scope: This specification sheet details the initialization parameters for astronomical clear-sky models for the High Plains Meridian location. The methodology defines the calculation of theoretical Global Horizontal Irradiance (GHI) by establishing a causal link between the established temporal window and the specific astronomical parameters required for irradiance simulation.

Simulation Window: A 72-hour period commencing at the start of the second week in February 2022.

Atmospheric Assumptions: The model assumes a pristine atmospheric state devoid of particulate or water vapor obstruction to facilitate theoretical clear-sky modeling.

Note: This document focuses strictly on modeling parameters and atmospheric assumptions for the February 2022 study. It does not include actual irradiance data results, weather forecasts, or site-specific historical climate data.
