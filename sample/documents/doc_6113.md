**MONITORING STATION ENGINEERING DEPARTMENT**
**Technical Maintenance Log: Sensor Calibration Discrepancy Report #4022**

**Subject:** Analysis of Anomalous Sensor Readings

**Diagnostic Summary:**
Engineering has completed an investigation into recent data inconsistencies. Findings indicate that the anomalous readings are a direct result of the experimental reflective cooling arrays currently deployed onsite. 

**Observation Data:**
Maintenance logs highlight specific misleading data points, characterized by unusually high values recorded during nighttime hours. These readings initially suggested irregular activity; however, technical auditing has confirmed a causal explanation linked to equipment calibration issues. 

**Conclusion:**
The identified spikes are not indicative of natural solar cycles or external environmental factors. Instead, the discrepancy originates from internal sensor miscalibration triggered by the reflective properties of the cooling hardware. Stationary monitoring equipment requires recalibration to account for array-induced interference.
