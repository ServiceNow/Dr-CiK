Nuance Cosmetic Lab
Sales and Revenue Operations Department
Internal Operational Report: Post-Campaign Promotional Performance Summary

I. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
This internal document provides a retrospective analysis of the operational adjustments and transaction trends observed during the recently concluded boost period. The report focuses on the immediate impact of the discount window on the sales volume of Nuance Cosmetic Lab and the subsequent restoration of standard pricing protocols.

II. TRANSACTIONAL VOLUME ANALYSIS
Data collected during the specific promotional window indicates that transaction volumes experienced a marginal upward trend. When evaluated against the previous baseline period, the increase in customer activity remained steady throughout the duration of the campaign, though the growth was characterized as incremental rather than a significant surge.

III. CAMPAIGN CONCLUSION AND OBJECTIVES
The promotional campaign was officially concluded according to the predefined operational timeline. This termination was executed once the primary sales objectives established for the boost period were successfully achieved. All operational benchmarks related to this specific window have been met, allowing the department to transition back to standard sales cycles.

IV. PRICING ADJUSTMENTS AND INCENTIVES
Following the expiration of the discount window, the following actions were taken to normalize the store’s revenue environment:

1. Removal of Incentives: All artificial purchase incentives introduced for the purpose of the campaign were removed immediately upon the conclusion of the promotional timeframe.
2. Reinstatement of Tiers: Previous pricing tiers were fully reinstated across the inventory. The store has returned to the pricing structure utilized prior to the commencement of the promotional period.

V. SCOPE LIMITATIONS
Departmental analysts note that this summary is strictly limited to the retrospective performance of the specific promotional window and the subsequent logistics of pricing adjustments. This report does not contain broader market forecasts, specific product-level nomenclature, or financial targets beyond the initial sales objectives mentioned in section III. Further inquiries regarding forward-looking revenue projections should be directed to the Strategic Planning division.
