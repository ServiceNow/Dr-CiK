1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
This report outlines the operational performance of Nuance Cosmetic Lab from the final quarter of 2025 through the conclusion of the 2026 fiscal year. Key developments include the strengthening of the leadership team, the expansion of our physical distribution footprint, and an analysis of sales and inventory velocity.

2. PERSONNEL & LEADERSHIP updates
Effective December 15, 2025 (2025-12-15), Nuance Cosmetic Lab finalized the hiring of a new Logistics Manager. This strategic appointment was intended to oversee the transition to expanded storage facilities and to optimize the direct-to-consumer fulfillment pipeline. The manager has been responsible for the implementation of the logistics protocols detailed in this review.

3. WAREHOUSING AND DISTRIBUTION
In early 2026, Nuance Cosmetic Lab officially opened its new local warehouse facility. This expansion addressed prior capacity constraints and allowed for localized stock positioning. 

Performance Impact: Following the activation of the local warehouse, the department observed a significant and sustained reduction in delivery times. By bypassing regional hubs and shipping directly from the local facility, the time elapsed between order placement and customer receipt has reached record lows for the company.
