2. COOLING SYSTEM TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

The facility utilizes a closed-loop chilled water system designed for high-density thermal management. 
- Infrastructure Type: Liquid-air hybrid cooling.
- Capacity: 1.2 Megawatts total heat rejection.
- Redundancy: N+1 configuration with dual centrifugal chillers.
- Pump Flow Rate: Constant flow primary/variable flow secondary.
- Operating Temperature: Maintained at 22°C (71.6°F) with a humidity range of 40-50%.
- Monitoring: Integrated thermal sensors installed at 12-inch intervals across the cold aisle containment ceiling.
