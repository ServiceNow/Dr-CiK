INTERNAL STRATEGIC MEMO

TO: Operations Department
FROM: Nuance Cosmetic Lab Operations Leadership
DATE: December 1, 2025
RE: Nuance Cosmetic Lab Year-End Pricing Schedule Update

This memorandum serves to document the strategic price reduction phase initiated by Nuance Cosmetic Lab at the conclusion of the 2025 calendar year. The organization has established a specific temporal framework to govern all discount activity associated with this initiative.

According to the approved schedule, the promotional markdown is officially scheduled to run from December 21st, 2025, until January 28th, 2026. All internal logistical operations must align with this timeframe. Please note that this document is intended exclusively to define the timing and organizational identification of the strategy; information regarding specific product lists, discount percentages, or projected financial outcomes will be issued via separate channels.
