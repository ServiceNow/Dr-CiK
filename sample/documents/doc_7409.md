Systems Engineering Department
Technical Report Summary: Blue Creek Technical Site Operations Update

Following the implementation of recent technical adjustments at the Blue Creek Technical Site, the Systems Engineering Department has completed a post-adjustment CPU performance analysis. 

Observations indicate that CPU load has successfully shifted into a steady, non-periodic progression. Usage levels have demonstrated a gradual and consistent recovery, ultimately stabilizing at a new, higher baseline. Current projections show that the load will remain at this higher plateau for the upcoming period. Based on the stability of the new operational state, significant downward fluctuations or returns to previous low levels are not expected at this time. This summary focuses exclusively on observed load patterns and stability metrics following the specified site adjustments.
