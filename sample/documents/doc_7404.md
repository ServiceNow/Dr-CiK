Real-time monitoring indicates that office climates are maintaining the standard set points required for employee comfort and hardware longevity.

The renovation of the staff breakroom at the Blue Creek Technical Site is progressing according to the current schedule. Demolition and utility rough-ins have been completed. III. Data Center Infrastructure: Cable Standards
To ensure uniformity during ongoing server maintenance and upgrades, all technicians must adhere to the following color coding specifications for ethernet cables used in the data center:

- Blue: General Data/Workstation Connectivity
- Red: Critical Uplinks/Backbone Infrastructure
- Yellow: Security and Surveillance Systems
- Green: Management and IPMI Ports
- White: Temporary/Testing Connections

IV. Backup Generator Maintenance
The quarterly backup generator testing is scheduled for Thursday, beginning at 02:00 AM. This is a standard load-test to verify power redundancy. Facility Operations confirms that this maintenance will be seamless; there is no expected impact on the Central Processing Unit (CPU) or active server computing tasks. The Automatic Transfer Switch (ATS) will manage power distribution to ensure continuous uptime.
