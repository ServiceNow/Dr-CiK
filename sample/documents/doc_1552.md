Post-Implementation Incident Resolution Report: Operational Script Migration

Following the identification of inflated sales volume data for Nuance Cosmetic Lab, the system received a comprehensive package of internal revisions to replace the legacy operational scripts. 

Verification logs confirmed that from the third of November onwards, the specific vulnerability causing the inflation was effectively neutralized. Implementation was finalized such that the system achieved stabilization as of November 3. Once the updated logic was active, the mechanism for the recurring bug was permanently removed. This intervention ensures that the scripts responsible for the manual data inflation are no longer present in the production environment, effectively closing the incident.
