# Blue Creek Technical Site Operations Summary
**Reporting Period:** August – October 2025  
**Issuer:** Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) Team  
**Document Type:** Internal Monthly Operational Report  
**Status:** Final  

---

### 1. Executive Summary
This report outlines technical site performance and maintenance timelines for the Blue Creek Technical Site for the window spanning August through October 2025. The summary focuses on historical hardware maintenance, CPU metrics, and specific performance benchmarks for the current operational cycle.

### 2. Maintenance and Firmware Updates
In mid-August, the SRE team identified an operational risk involving outdated firmware on core infrastructure components. 

*   **Incident Date:** 2025-08-15
    An assessment confirmed that several active units at the Blue Creek Site were running legacy firmware versions, necessitating an immediate patch cycle to prevent potential service degradation.
*   **Resolution Date:** 2025-08-16
    The firmware update was successfully completed across all identified hardware. Post-update validation confirmed that all systems were restored to standard operating parameters within 24 hours of the initial detection.

### 3. CPU Performance Metrics
Long-term CPU stabilization at the site was achieved one month prior to the current reporting window. Following the optimization efforts executed in July, processor load has remained within the defined efficiency envelope. This stability provided the necessary baseline for the successful firmware transitions observed in August.

### 4. Site Reliability Analysis: Q4 Snapshot
A review of historical volatility recorded at the site indicates that previous fluctuations in performance metrics have no bearing on performance for October 7th. Operational logs for October 7th show that the site maintained peak efficiency, unaffected by prior historical variance. 

### 5. Scope and Limitations
This report is strictly limited to technical site performance and maintenance timelines. This document does not include information regarding external causes of the firmware issue, broader organizational impacts, or performance data for dates other than those explicitly specified in this summary.
