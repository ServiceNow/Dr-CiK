Specific morning (09:00) and afternoon (15:00) radiation values serve as buffers surrounding the midday peak to demonstrate the theoretical trend across the tracking period.

This source is limited to recorded radiation measurements and the immediate orbital explanation for observed trends; it does not include long-term weather forecasts, equipment maintenance logs, or broader climate change analysis.
